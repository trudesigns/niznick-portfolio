Dr. Gerald Niznick
A 40 year Legacy of Dental Implant Research, Innovation, Education and Philanthropy

1982-1997 Core-Vent Corp
Dr. Niznick launched the Core-Vent System in 1982 and Screw-Vent in 1986(Sold by Zimmer Biomet today) Screw-Vent’s Patented Internal Conical Connection (#9,960,381) is the Cornerstone of Modern Implant Design

1997-2001 Paragon Implant Co
Core-Vent renamed Paragon with launch of Tapered Screw-Vent (1-Stage) and Paragon Implants (Healing Collar Packaging). December 2001: Sold Paragon to Calcitec > Zimmer > Zimmer Biomet

2004 - 2010 Implant Direct LLC
Dr. Niznick founded Implant Direct (ID) 2004 and launched Spectra-System, RePlant and Legacy Implants. Sybron bought 75% of ID Dec. 2010 & remaining 25% Jan. 2014 after Dr. Niznick resigned as President.

2011 - Present: Implant Direct

Dr. Niznick founded Implant Direct (ID) 2004 and launched Spectra-System, RePlant and Legacy Implants. Sybron bought 75% of ID Dec. 2010 & remaining 25% Jan. 2014 after Dr. Niznick resigned as President.
