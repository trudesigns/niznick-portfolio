Since 1982, I have been speaking out on the many controversies and issues that affect implant dentistry. Editorials, open letters to the profession and e-mail exchanges have been the forums for these discussions. The first major controvery arose in 1984 with Nobel's open letter to Oral Surgeons and Prosthodontists objecting to Core-Vent's references to and extrapolation of Branemark's research. I believe you will find these discussions informative and entertaining.
