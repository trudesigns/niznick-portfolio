Dr. Gerald Niznick

A 40 year Legacy of Dental Implant Research, Innovation, Education and Philanthropy

1982-1997 Core-Vent Corp

Dr. Niznick launched the Core-Vent System in 1982 and Screw-Vent in 1986(Sold by Zimmer Biomet today) Screw-Vent’s Patented
Internal Conical Connection (#9,960,381) is the Cornerstone of Modern Implant Design

1997-2001 Paragon Implant Co

Core-Vent renamed Paragon with launch of Tapered Screw-Vent (1-Stage) and Paragon Implants (Healing Collar Packaging).
December 2001: Sold Paragon to Calcitec > Zimmer > Zimmer Biomet

2004 - 2010 Implant Direct LLC

Dr. Niznick founded Implant Direct LLC in 2004. He developed and patented a number of new implants with All-in-1 packaging,
selling high quality products on-line for unprecedented low prices, thus creating the Value Segment of the Implant Industry

2011 - Present: Implant Direct Sybron

Dr. Niznick sold 75% of Implant Direct LLC December 2010 and his remaining 25% January 2014 to Sybron/Danaher. He remained as president
through 2013 launching the Legacy4, InterActive Implant Systems and cad-milled abutments/bars
